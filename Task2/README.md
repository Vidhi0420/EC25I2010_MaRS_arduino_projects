Task - 2 : Changing the direction of a motor using a pushbutton and controlling the speed using a regulator

Components used:

Arduino Uno

DC Motor

Pushbutton

Potentiometer

L293D Motor Driver

Connecting Wires


Connections:

L293D:

The Enable 1 & 2 pin is connected to pin 11

Input1 is connected to pin 10

Input2 is connected to pin 9

Output1 is connected to Terminal 1 of the motor

Output2 is connected to Terminal 2 of the motor

The ground pins are connected to ground


The power1 pin is connected to 5V
